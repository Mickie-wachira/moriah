About Mt. Moriah Branch

“For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them.” — Matthew 18:20

Our Story & Purpose

Mt. Moriah Branch is a vibrant church family under the Full Gospel Churches of Kenya (FGCK). We exist to share the Gospel of Christ, build strong families, and transform our community through worship, teaching, and service.

Purpose of this Website
  • • Mobilize contributions in Ksh for church construction and community projects.
  • • Show live progress updates of the construction (photos, milestones, percentages).
  • • Share messages & Bible verses from our leadership on the homepage.
  • • Optionally recognize top contributors and testimonies (with consent).
